# 💸 Costly Habit Calculator

> Find out what your daily habits are **really** costing you — over 10, 20, or 30 years.

A free, private, browser-based tool by **[Costly Choices](https://www.youtube.com/@Costly_Choices_Official)** — the YouTube channel exposing how corporations engineer your spending.

---

## What It Does

Enter any daily habit — coffee, cigarettes, takeout, subscriptions — and instantly see:

- The **true lifetime cost** over up to 40 years
- Your **yearly and monthly** spend broken down
- The **inflation-adjusted** real cost in today's money
- What that money would be worth if **invested instead**
- A visual **growth chart** comparing spending vs. investing
- Concrete comparisons — flights, rent months, emergency fund

## Why It's Safe to Use
